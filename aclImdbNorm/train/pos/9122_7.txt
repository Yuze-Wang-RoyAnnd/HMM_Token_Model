purple rain is a great film if you are a 40-something music lover who partied to prince music in the 1980s. the story is a much-used formula: rising musical talent must overcome personal and professional hurdles to find his audience and self-respect. prince is a bad actor, but his performance, especially of purple rain/i would die 4 u make it worth sitting through the film. he is himself and as a showman, as powerful on stage as mick jagger or michael jackson, circa thriller. apollonia kotero, a former prince prot'eg'e, is beautiful and shows some acting talent and chemistry with prince, but her script doesn't let her far. morris day and other supporting actors provide comedic elements.
