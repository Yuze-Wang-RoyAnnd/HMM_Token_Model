seven months since a revelatory viewing of faces, i finally found a rentable dvd copy of cassavetes' first feature. shot on a shoestring in manhattan and in his acting workshop on ad hoc sets, shadows was the culmination of months of improvisational rehearsals, in which the (mostly amateur) actors developed bonds with one another, invented their characters, and polished their techniques to give their filmed performances just the right tenor of spontaneous familiarity. this intimate approach led to some incredibly daring work in faces
