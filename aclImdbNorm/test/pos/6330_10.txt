bergman is a sublime comedy director and writer. this fact becomes apparent in "en lektion i k"arlek", where the comic elements range from pure slapstick to deep, yet very emotional scenes. this movie is paves the way for bergman's later comedies "sommarnattens leende" and "kvinnodr"om", all of them starring gunnar bj"ornstrand as well as eva dahlbeck. this is an excellent movie with which to start your bergman experience, acutely portraying emotional troubles of the young as well as the old. the cinematography by martin bodin is astounding, for instance in the picnic scene. in short, the movie is a perfect example of a successful comedy, with a clarity of depth even surpassing some of bergman's own comedies.
