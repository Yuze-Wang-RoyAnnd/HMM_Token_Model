linda arvidson (as jennie) and harry solter (as frank) are enjoying a romantic tryst, when in walks her father charles inslee; furious, he chases mr. solter out of the house. undaunted, he goes to her balcony and begs her to elope. ms. arvidson is agreeable, and goes to pack. then, burglar george gebhardt arrives to rob the place. though he doesn't get much in the way of booty, thieving gebhardt manages to use arvidson's trunk to escape from the police
