in the mountains of japan, forlorn young artist sessue hayakawa (as tatsu aka "the dragon painter") paints magnificent landscapes. he prays "divinity" will restore his fianc'ee, whom he believes was changed into a dragon, 1,000 years ago. meanwhile, in tokyo, an forlorn older painter, edward peil (as kano indara) laments not having a son to carry on his family line of artists. when mr. peil sees mr. hayakawa's paintings, he sees a painter worthy to become his "son and disciple." hayakawa also falls in love with peil's daughter tsuru aoki (ume-ko), believing she's the reincarnation of his long lost princess. but, with his love fulfilled, hayakawa loses his ability to paint
