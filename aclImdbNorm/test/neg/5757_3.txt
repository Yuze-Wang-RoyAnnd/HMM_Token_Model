director and fx man john carl buechler doesn't have to do much in order to terrify me; the sight of his name in the credits alone is enough to strike fear into my heart.<br /><br />his lamentable straight-to-video output in the 80s sat on the bottom shelf of the horror section at my local rental shop; twenty years later, and his dvds occupy the same space. it seems that the more things change, the more they stay the same. you can rely on old jcb to serve up dreck, whatever the format and regardless of advances in movie-making technology.<br /><br />in this contemptible offering, a bunch of friends travel to a remote town where they discover the secret treasure hoard of jeremiah stone, aka the forty-niner
